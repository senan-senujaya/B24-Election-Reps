📊 Voting Mechanics Explained

Because the number of nominees differs from the required representatives per degree, specific voting limits are enforced to determine the Main Representatives.

🧠 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Vote for 1

Both nominees are guaranteed a spot, but your vote decides the Main AI Rep.

💻 Information Technology (IT) Varying Limits

  • Males (Vote for 2): All 5 boys have secured a spot (we need 5), but this vote determines the Main Batch Rep . He will also be the IT main Rep.
  • Females (Vote Top 3): Out of the 5 nominees, 3 will be selected.

👩‍💻 Information Technology & Management (ITM) Varying Limits

  • Males (Vote for 1): All 3 boys have secured a spot (we need 3), but this decides the Main ITM Rep.
  • Females (Vote Top 2): Out of the 3 nominees, 2 will be selected.
